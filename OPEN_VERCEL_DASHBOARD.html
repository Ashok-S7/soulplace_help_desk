Get your Soulplace login link

Do not type "your-real-url" in the browser. That is not a real address. Follow the steps below.

  1. Click the button below to open your Vercel dashboard.
  2. Sign in if needed, then click your Soulplace project.
  3. On the project page, click the Visit button. Your app will open.
  4. Look at the address bar. You will see something like https://something.vercel.app. That is your real URL.
  5. Add /soulplace/login to that URL and press Enter. That is your login link. Bookmark it.
