================================================================================
  SOULPLACE HELP DESK – HOW TO GET YOUR LOGIN LINK (Vercel + Chrome)
================================================================================

  *** DO NOT type "your-real-url" or "YOUR-REAL-URL" in the browser. ***
  *** That is only a placeholder. Your real link comes from Vercel. ***

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  STEP 1 – Open your app on Vercel
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. Open Chrome and go to:   https://vercel.com/dashboard
2. Sign in if needed.
3. Click your Soulplace project (e.g. "soulplace_help_desk" or the name you gave it).
4. On the project page, click the "Visit" button (or the main link under the project name).
5. Your app will open. The address bar will show your REAL URL, for example:
   https://soulplace-help-desk-abc123.vercel.app
   or
   https://soulplace_help_desk-username.vercel.app

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  STEP 2 – Your actual links (use the same domain you see in Step 1)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
In the address bar, you will see something like:
   https://something.vercel.app

Your links are (replace "something.vercel.app" with what YOU see):

  • Staff / Admin LOGIN:
    https://something.vercel.app/soulplace/login

  • Page to copy all links (with API token):
    https://something.vercel.app/soulplace/links

  • Tables (QR codes):
    https://something.vercel.app/soulplace/tables

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  STEP 3 – Bookmark or copy
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
• After you click "Visit" in Step 1, type /soulplace/login at the end of the URL
  and press Enter. That page is your login. Bookmark it.
• Or go to /soulplace/links and use "Copy" there to get every link with the
  API token already in it.

================================================================================
  Example (your URL will be different – get it from Vercel Visit button)
================================================================================
If Vercel shows your site at:  https://soulplace-help-desk-xyz.vercel.app

Then your login link is:       https://soulplace-help-desk-xyz.vercel.app/soulplace/login
